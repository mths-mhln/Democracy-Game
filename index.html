The Republic of Civitas

An Interactive Democracy Simulation

The thunderous applause echoes through the Great Assembly Hall as you take the oath of office. You are now Prime Minister of the Republic of Civitas—a nation of 10 million citizens emerging from decades of authoritarian rule. The people have placed their hopes in you to build something unprecedented for them: a lasting democracy.

But democracy is not a destination—it's a daily choice. Around you, the forces of history watch and wait. Military generals who remember the "efficiency" of the old regime. Economic elites worried about their privileges. Social movements demanding immediate change. Foreign powers with their own agendas. The international community, hopeful yet skeptical.

Your every decision can send ripples through five critical pillars of democratic governance: Public Trust in your leadership, Economic Prosperity that legitimizes the system, Social Cohesion that binds citizens together, Institutional Strength that outlasts any single leader, and Military Loyalty to civilian authority.

Tread carefully: History is littered with democracies that crumbled under the weight of poor decisions. Will you be remembered as the architect of a thriving republic, or as the leader who let freedom slip away? The choice—and the responsibility—are yours.
